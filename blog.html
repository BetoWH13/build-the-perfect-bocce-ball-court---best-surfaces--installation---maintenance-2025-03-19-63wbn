How to Build the Perfect Bocce Ball Court: A Complete Guide

March 19, 2025

Transform your backyard into a professional bocce destination with this comprehensive guide to court construction. Learn the essential steps from initial planning and proper dimensions to selecting premium materials like crushed stone base and decomposed granite. The guide covers critical aspects including ground preparation, surface layering, and drainage installation for optimal playability. Discover maintenance best practices to keep your court in top condition, plus solutions to common challenges like surface pudding and uneven wear. With expert tips on enhancement features like lighting and seating areas, you'll create a lasting recreational space for years of enjoyment.
